APICTA International Awards 2017

Divine IT Limited was awarded “ Merit” for the product PrismERP retail edition in RETA -10 category on 10th December 2017. The award acknowledges the innovativeness of the product through which it caters any size of retail business. The recognition elevated the company’s level of capacity for serving solutions globally. The Asia Pacific ICT Alliance Awards (Oscar of ICT) is an international award program organized by APICTA, which aims to elevate ICT awareness in the region and provides the platform for the tech- innovators to showcase their inventive creations.
